Apex Gaming Invitational Info

The Apex Gaming Invitational Series is a Magic: the Gathering series hosted by participating local game stores. The Invitational will be hosted at Apex Gaming on Nov 19th, 2023! You might be asking, "What are the details and how do I qualify?" Check out the info below:

What is the Invitational

  • Invite only, (zero entry fee) Modern MTG tournament.
  • Swiss tournament structure with a cute to top 8. Top 8 will be best of 3 with the finals being best of 5. Higher sees will get choice of play/draw. Top 8 will be open decklist.
  • $20,000 in prizes!
  • Trophy will be awarded to the champion at the conclusion of the Invitational.

How do I qualify for the Invitational?

  • Play at any Apex Invitational Qualifier (AIQ) at any of the participating stores to win qualifications to the Invitational in November.
  • The top 2 finishers will get an invite to the Invitational (if someone is already qualified it will pass to the next highest non-qualified finisher of that tournament).
  • There will be three last chance qualifiers (LCQs). One on Friday, Nov 17th and two on Saturday, Nov 18th. Friday, Nov 17th will be a Modern LCQ starting at 5pm. Saturday will be a Modern LCQ starting at 11am with a Pioneer LCQ starting at 5pm. Each of these events will be 5 rounds of swiss awarding a minimum of 4 spots each to the Invitational.
  • The Season 2 winner already recieved an invite to the Season 3 Invitational. The Season 3 winner will be invited to the Season 4 Invitational.
  • Formats and payouts of the individual AIQs will be determined by the hosting store.
